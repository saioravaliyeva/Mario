MARIO





GAMES

Super Mario Bros.

A platforming video game developed and released in 1985 by the Japanese company Nintendo for the Famicom platform. Listed in the Guinness Book of Records as the best-selling game in history.

Super Mario Land

A platformer video game developed and published by Nintendo for the handheld Game Boy console. This is the first Mario platformer released for a handheld console. Gameplay is generally similar to that of Super Mario Bros. 1985, but the mechanics were modified to fit the screen size of the smaller device.

Super Mario World

A platformer released by Nintendo for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES) in 1990. The game was produced by Nintendo Entertainment Analysis and Development under the direction of Shigeru Miyamoto and Takashi Tezuka. Super Mario World has made its way to the SNES launch lineup.

Super Mario 3D World

A video game from the Mario series developed by Nintendo EAD Tokyo for the Nintendo Wii U platform. It was released on November 21, 2013 in Japan, November 22 in the US and North America, November 26 in Brazil and South America, November 29 in Europe, and Russia, and November 30 - in Australia. The game was first presented at the annual Electronic Entertainment Expo in Los Angeles. It is the sequel to Super Mario 3D Land for the Nintendo 3DS.

Super Mario Odyssey

A video game from the Mario series of games developed by the Nintendo EPD for the Nintendo Switch game console. The game is a 3D platformer and its gameplay is more close to Super Mario 64 and Super Mario Sunshine, allowing you to freely explore open game worlds.

Super Mario Maker 2

It is a platform-based computer game and level editor developed by Nintendo and released exclusively for the Nintendo Switch game console on June 28, 2019.
